Meet the Man of the Moment: Governor Alex Otti

Politics • July 12, 2025

Meet the Man of the Moment: Governor Alex Otti Governor Alex Chioma Otti hails from Umuehim Village, Ehi na Uguru Ancient Kingdom in Isialangwa South Local Government Area of Abia State. Born on February 18, 1965, to Pastor and Mrs. Lazarus Weze Otti, he attended Ngwa High School, Abayi Aba, and Secondary Technical School, Okpuala Ngwa, both in Abia State. Otti recorded the best result in his school's certificate examination for his set and graduated from the University of Port Harcourt in 1988 with First Class Honours in Economics. He began his banking career in 1989 at Nigerian International Bank (Citibank), then moved to Nigerian Intercontinental Merchant Bank LTD, and later joined Societe Bancaire Nigeria Limited (Banque SBA, Paris) as Senior Manager in 1992. While working, he earned an MBA from the University of Lagos in 1994 and attended international courses in Columbia and the United States (Pennsylvania), as well as an executive programme at INSEAD, Fountainebleau, France. Otti later joined UBA as Principal Manager, growing the bank's oil and gas business. In May 2001, he joined First Bank of Nigeria Plc as Assistant General Manager for the energy sector, holding several executive positions including Deputy General Manager, Executive Director Commercial Banking, and Executive Director, South Directorate. In March 2011, he became Group Managing Director/CEO of Diamond Bank, with his tenure renewed in 2014 for his significant impact. The Chairman of Diamond Bank, Igwe Nnaemeka Alfred Achebe, described Otti's years as the brightest in the bank's history. Otti retired from banking in October 2014 and entered politics with APGA, contesting for Abia State Governor in 2015 and 2019. Despite setbacks, his third attempt in 2023 on the Labour Party platform was successful, and he was sworn in as Governor. Today, Governor Alex Chioma Otti is celebrated for his productivity and impact in Abia State. He is married to Priscilla Chidimma Otti, and their union is blessed with three children. May God bless him.
