Meet the Man of the Moment: Governor Alex Otti

Politics • July 12, 2025

Governor Alex Chioma Otti hails from Umuehim Village, Ehi na Uguru Ancient Kingdom in Isialangwa South Local Government Area of Abia State.

He was born on February 18, 1965, to Pastor and Mrs Lazarus Weze Otti.

He had his secondary education at Ngwa High School, Abayi Aba, and Secondary Technical School, Okpuala Ngwa, both in Abia State.

Otti recorded the best result in his school's school certificate examination for his set.

In 1988, he graduated from the University of Portharcourt, Nigeria, with a First Class Honours Degree in Economics.

In 1989, Otti started job as a banker with the Nigerian International Bank, a subsidiary of Citibank, New York, where he worked in the Operations Department.

He later moved to the then Nigerian Intercontinental Merchant Bank LTD.

In 1992, Otti joined Societe Bancaire Nigeria Limited, a subsidiary of Banque SBA, Paris, rising to the position of a Senior Manager.

While doing his banking job, Otti obtained an MBA degree from the University of Lagos in 1994.

He attended various international courses in Columbia and in the United States, (Pennsylvania).

He also did an executive programme at INSEAD, Fount.
